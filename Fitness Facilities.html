Fitness Facilities


All Fitness Centers are open to all current faculty, staff and students. A current NOVACard is required.

All persons wishing to use the center must complete an orientation session and sign a waiver.

Fitness Centers are for workouts, intramurals, and college athletic events only.

Loitering is not permitted at any Fitness Center.
